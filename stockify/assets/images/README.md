# Assets Folder

Place your logo and product images here.

## Required Images

### Logo
- **File name**: `logo.png`
- **Recommended size**: 512x512 px or larger
- **Format**: PNG with transparent background
- **Usage**: Welcome screen, Login screen, Signup screen

### Product Images
Add product images with descriptive names, for example:
- `creme.png`
- `lipstick.png`
- `foundation.png`
- etc.

## Image Guidelines

1. **Logo**: Should be square or have equal padding for circular display
2. **Product Images**: Should be clear, well-lit photos on white/transparent background
3. **Format**: PNG preferred for transparency, JPG for photos
4. **Size**: Keep images under 2MB for better app performance
5. **Resolution**: At least 512x512 for logo, 800x800 for products

---

**Note**: After adding images, run `flutter pub get` to ensure assets are properly loaded.
